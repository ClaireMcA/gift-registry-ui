FAQ

When should I arrive?

The ceremony will start promptly at 4:30pm so please arrive well before this time. If you arrive at or after this time, we please ask you to refrain from entering until the processional has finished.


When should I leave?

We are required to have all guests departed from the venue by 11:30pm so please bear that in mind.


What should I wear?

This event will be a formal event. Please take this as a chance to get out your fanciest clothes and dress to impress. Please bear in mind that the ceremony and cocktail hour take place on lawns so skinny heels may not be the best choice. March in Adelaide can vary quite a bit weather-wise so please check the weather forecast and remember to bring a jacket if it looks like it will be cold!


Will I have to leave the venue between the Ceremony and the Reception?

No, you won’t! Our Ceremony will be followed directly by a catered cocktail hour and then straight into the reception.


Can I bring my kids?

As much as we both love children, this will be a child free event aside from those children in the bridal party. Please take this as an opportunity to have the night off! There is no “waiting time” between the ceremony and reception (aside from a catered cocktail hour) and the event takes place all in the one location, making having children at the ceremony only also quite difficult. We apologise for any inconvenience this causes.


I'm coming from interstate, do you have any recommendation of where I should stay?

Please bear in mind that March can be a busy time of year for Adelaide as we have many festivals and the Adelaide Fringe on at this time. Please book accommodation as soon as you are able to. There are a couple of hotels in Mawson Lakes (about 30 min from the venue) and a number of Airbnb’s located around the area.
