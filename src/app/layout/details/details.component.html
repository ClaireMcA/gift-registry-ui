FAQ

What should I wear?
The wedding will be semi-formal. The ceremony will be indoors but the reception will be in a sheltered outdoor space.


Can I bring the kids?
We welcome your children to the ceremony and look forward to sharing our celebration with them! However, we have decided to keep the reception child-free except for those in our family. We hope you can understand this decision and enjoy a night off from the rascals! If you are having trouble finding a babysitter, please let us know.


What can I do between the ceremony and reception?
Yass has some nice spaces for you to explore. You can take a stroll down the main street and check out the little niche stores, meander along Yass River through Riverbank Park, grab a coffee at one of the many local cafes (Trader & Co is a favourite!) or head on over to The Clubhouse Hotel to grab a drink before the party begins.


I am travelling from afar and will need accomodation. Any suggestions?
There are a number of motels in Yass if you'd like accommodation close to the festivities. Alternatively, there are many accommodation options in Canberra. Belconnen or a northern suburb of Canberra will be an optimal location to get onto the Barton Highway to head out to Yass. Yass is about a 45 minute drive from Canberra, so if you are flying down and need help with transport, let us know!
